Devices

By clicking on the "Devices" button on "Home", you can access your devices by their type, such as Alarms, Doors, ACs, Refrigerators, Lamps, Ovens or Blinds. You can also access your devices all together in the All category or access only your favorites.

Rooms

By clicking on the "Rooms" button on "Home", you can access your devices according to the room where they are located. In each room you will see a list of all the devices placed in that room. At first, no rooms are displayed, so you will have to add rooms to see them. If devices with no room are added, an "Ungrouped" section is added.

Routines

By clicking on the "Routines" button on "Home", you can access the routines that were created to be played, edited or deleted. You can also create new ones.

Menu

By clicking on the TechHaus logo, you will be redirected to the "Home" page.

Settings

By clicking on the "Settings" button, a list is displayed where you can access the help menu.

Rooms

You can access your devices organized by rooms.

Adding rooms

You can add rooms by clicking on the '+' icon that appears next to "Rooms". This will display a popup to add the room information.

Deleting rooms

You can delete rooms by clicking on the trashcan icon that appears if you move the mouse over it. This will display a popup to warn you that the action will delete all the devices associated with the room and ask for confirmation.

Editing room name

You can edit room names by entering the room and clicking on its name. A pencil icon will appear when you move the mouse over it. Clicking the room's name will display a box where you can type a new name. Finally, press enter to save or click outside the box.

Type of devices

You can access your devices by categories according to the type of device. There are 8 categories: Alarms, Doors, ACs, Refrigerators, Lamps, Ovens and Blinds.

Favorites

You can access your favorite devices in this section. Your favorite devices will be those that were marked by a heart-shaped button.

All

You can access all your devices in this section regardless of the type of device or the room in which they are located.

Adding devices

To add a device, you can go to its category or to the room where it will be located. Once this is done, click on the button next to the name of the section that has the '+' symbol. This will open a popup window to complete with the information of the new device.

Deleting devices

To remove a device, go to a section where the device appears: its room, its category, the All section or Favorites (if applicable). Then click on the trashcan icon next to it.

Mark as favorite

To mark or unmark a device as favorite, go to a section where the device appears: its room, its category or the All section. Then color the corresponding heart icon. If you do it from the Favorites section, you will have to reload the page to see the changes.

Editing devices names

To edit a device name, go to a section where the device appears: its room, its category or the All section or Favorites. Move the mouse on the name until a pencil icon appears. Click on the name and this will display an input where you can insert a new name. Finally, press enter to save or click outside the input.

Devices shorcut status

You can see the status of the device using its shortcut located in the bar. In addition, you can press the icon to change its status. This is not applicable for the refrigerator, which only shows the refrigerator's temperature.

Devices all status

You can see the entire state of the device by pressing on the bar. This will open a panel within the list where all its features are displayed. In addition, you can interact with each of the functionalities of the device.

Play Routines

You can play your routines by clicking on the play icon next to its name. This will display a black label on the bottom of the page warning you that the routine was played. After this, the status of the devices involved in the routine will change according to the action executed.

Routines details

You can access the details of the routines by clicking on the bar. This expands a panel that will show the devices involved and the actions it will execute if it is played.

Adding routines

You can add routines by clicking on the '+' icon that appears next to "Routines". This will display a popup to add the routine information.

Deleting routines

You can delete routines by clicking on the trashcan icon that appears in the bar. This will display a popup to confirm the action.

Editing routines

You can edit routines by clicking on the pencil icon that appears in the bar. This will display a popup to add one device and an action to it.
