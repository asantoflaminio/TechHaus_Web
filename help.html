Devices

By clicking on the "Devices" button on "Home", you can access your devices by their type, such as Alarms, Doors, Air Conditioners, Fridges, Lights, Ovens, Curtains or Temporizers. You can also access your devices all together in the All category or access only your favorites.

Rooms

By clicking on the "Rooms" button on "Home", you can access your devices according to the room where they are located. In each room you will see a list that includes all the devices placed in that room. At first, there will be no room loaded in the system, so you will have to create one.

Routines

By clicking on the "Routines" button on "Home", you can access the routines that were created to be played, edited or deleted. You can also create new ones.

Menu

By clicking on the TechHaus logo, you will be redirected to the "Home" page.

Settings

By clicking on the "Settings" button, two options are displayed where you can access the profiles or help panel.

Rooms

You can access your devices organized by rooms.

Adding rooms

You can add rooms by clicking on the '+' icon that appears next to "Rooms". This will display a popup to add the room information.

Deleting rooms

You can delete rooms by clicking on the trashcan icon that appears if you move the mouse on it. This will display a popup to warning you the action will delete all the devices associated with the room and ask for confirmation.

Editing room name

You can edit rooms name by entering the room and clicking on the name after a pencil icon appears when you move the mouse on it. This will display an input where you can insert a new name. Finally, press enter to save or click outside the input.

Type of devices

You can access your devices by categories according to the type of device. There are 8 categories such as Alarms, Doors, Air Conditioners, Fridges, Lights, Ovens, Curtains and Temporizers.

Favorites

You can access your favorite devices in this section. Your favorite devices will be those that were marked by a heart-shaped button.

All

You can access all your devices in this section regardless of the type of device or the room in which they are located.

Adding devices

To add a device, you can go to the category of it or room where it will be located. Once this is done, click on the button next to the name of the section that has the '+' symbol. This will open a popup window to complete with the information of the new device.

Deleting devices

To remove a device, go to the section where the device appears, whether it's room, it's category, the All section or Favorites(if applicable). Then click on the trashcan icon next to it.

Mark as favorite

To mark a device as a favorite, go to the section where the device appears whether it's room, it's category or the All section. Then color the corresponding heart icon. If you do it from the Favorites section, you will have to reload the page to see the changes.

Editing devices names

To edit a device name, go to the section where the device appears whether it's room, it's category, the All section or Favorites. Move the mouse on the name until a pencil icon appears. Click on the name and this will display an input where you can insert a new name. Finally, press enter to save or click outside the input.

Devices shorcut status

You can see the status of the device using its shortcut located in the bar. In addition, you can press the icon to change its status. This is not applicable for the Fridge.

Devices all status

You can see the entire state of the device by pressing on the bar. This will open a panel in the list where all the features are displayed. In addition, you can interact with each of the functionalities of it.

Play Routines

You can play your routines by clicking on the play icon next to its name. This will display a black label on the bottom of the page warning you that the routine was played. After this, the status of the devices involved in the routine will change according to the action executed.

Routines details

You can access the details of the routines by clicking on the bar. This expands a panel that will show the devices and the actions it will execute if it's played.

Adding routines

You can add routines by clicking on the '+' icon that appears next to "Routines". This will display a popup to add the routine information.

Deleting routines

You can delete routines by clicking on the trashcan icon that appears in the bar. This will display a popup to confirm the action.

Editing routines

You can edit routines by clicking on the pencil icon that appears in the bar. This will display a popup to add one device and an action to it.
