Favorites category

You can select devices as favorites so you can get access to them quicklier. Devices marked as favorites will be displayed in the "Favorites" category under the "Devices" tab.

Mark as favorite

To mark a device as favorite go to a section where the device is listed and then colour the heart next to it by clicking on it.

Devices

Under the 'Devices' tab you can access you devices by categories. Click on the one you want too see a list. You can add new devices on each category.

Rooms

Under the 'Rooms' tab you can access your devices organized by rooms. You can create more rooms using the add button. You can also add new devices in each room.

Routines

Under the 'Routines' tab you can access your routines, create new ones and modify them. To execute a routine click on the play icon next to its name. You can see details of the routine by expanding it.

Deleting devices

To delete a device, go to a section where the device is listed, whether it's a room or a device category. Then click on the trash can icon next to it.

Deleting rooms

To delete a room go to the 'Rooms' tab and by passing the cursor over a room a trash can icon will appear. Click on it to delete the selected room.

Adding devices, rooms and routines

To add a device you can go to a device category or a specific room. Then click on the add icon to add a new device. A similar button is found under the 'Rooms' tab to add a room and under 'Routines' to create a routine.
